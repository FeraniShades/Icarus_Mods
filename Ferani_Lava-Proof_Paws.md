If lava is so dangerous, why does it look so enticing??

Mounts and juveniles aren't the greatest at pathing, and sometimes try to follow you... right into a pool of lava. Now they will no longer catch on fire from lava and die if they touch it, and you can safely let them roam free in volcanic areas without keeping a fire extinguisher on you at all times. 

Used files:
D_ModifierStates

If you have any other mods that affect lava, they might conflict, so make sure to check.

Feel free to message me if you come across any bugs that need patching.

Installation:

WITH ICARUS MOD MANAGER by JIMK72 (RECOMMENDED):

Download the EXMODZ-file
Open your Mod Manager
On the right side, choose "Import EXMODZ File"
Choose the EXMODZ-file you just downloaded (extract it from ZIP), and it will show up on the list on the left
Add it to your list of "Mods to Install" by double-clicking on the mod name
Merge it by itself or with other mods (there may be conflicts, so keep an eye on what is being merged)
Play!
Feedback and suggestions are welcome!
