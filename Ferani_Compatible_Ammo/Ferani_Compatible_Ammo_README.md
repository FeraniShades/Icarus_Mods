All ammunition is compatible with all firearms.

Any ammunition is compatible with any firearm (except for flares and the flare gun). Any ammunition can be used for turrets. Arrows and bolts can be used by both bows and crossbows.

Used files:
D_ValidAmmoTypes
D_FirearmData
D_Turret

If you have any other mods that affect ammunition in the game, it might conflict, so make sure to check.

WITH ICARUS MOD MANAGER by JIMK72 (RECOMMENDED):

Download the EXMODZ-file
Open your Mod Manager
On the right side, choose "Import EXMODZ File"
Choose the EXMODZ-file you just downloaded (extract it from ZIP), and it will show up on the list on the left
Add it to your list of "Mods to Install" by double-clicking on the mod name
Merge it by itself or with other mods (there may be conflicts, so keep an eye on what is being merged)
Play!

WITHOUT MOD MANAGER:

Download the PAK-file
Navigate to your Icarus-folder (Example: C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\Icarus)
Go to \Icarus\Content\Paks\ods. If you don't have a "Mods" folder, you can create one
Insert the PAK-file (not a ZIP) in "Mods"
Play!
