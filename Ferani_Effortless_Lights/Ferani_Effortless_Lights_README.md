Ferani's Effortless Lights

No more stressing over cables and fuel!
All powered lights work without a power supply (except for battery-powered lights). They still have an outlet for a cable, so they will stay on your old power grid even if you disable the mod. Cuts down on hours and hours of cable management!
All candles can be activated with no fuel in them, simply click "Activate"!

Used files:
D_Energy
D_Generator
If you have any other mods that affect the files above, it might conflict, so make sure to check.

Feel free to message me if you come across any bugs that need patching.

Installation:
WITH ICARUS MOD MANAGER by JIMK72 (RECOMMENDED):
Download the EXMODZ-file
Open your Mod Manager
On the right side, choose "Import EXMODZ File"
Choose the EXMODZ-file you just downloaded (extract it from ZIP), and it will show up on the list on the left
Add it to your list of "Mods to Install" by double-clicking on the mod name
Merge it by itself or with other mods (there may be conflicts, so keep an eye on what is being merged)
Play!
